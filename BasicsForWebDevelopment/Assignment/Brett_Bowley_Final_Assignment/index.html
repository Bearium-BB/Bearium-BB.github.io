What do you do as a game developer

What it means to be a game developer. you take people's ideas or your own and turn them into games. but most of the time you will be working with a team of people to make a game. To make a full game by yourself is very hard. you have to be good at several things like a 3D modeler, Programming, and a music designer. You have to be good at all three of those. to make a small game. You need to work well as a team. It's not hard to get good at one of those things. It just takes a bunch of time and a lot of practice.
